Loading Indicator

Source: Figma · Reference: Clay docs. Default .loading-animation = circle (ring) spinner — three sides washed at ~25%, top quadrant full colour, rotating 360°. Add .loading-animation-squares for the paired-squares animation (cards, modals). Sizes -xs/-sm/-md/-lg; colours -primary/-secondary/-light. Always aria-hidden="true" — convey state via the surrounding region.
