Let's tailor your Liferay experience

Liferay and selected third-party providers use cookies and similar technologies to keep this site secure, help it run smoothly, and understand how it is used so we can improve it. You can accept them all or keep only what is essential. See our Privacy Policy.

Choosing "Accept All" lets us and these third parties place all cookies and similar tools and process the related data, including usage, device, and approximate location data, for personalization, analytics, and performance, as described in our Privacy Policy.

Choosing "Decline All" keeps only the cookies strictly needed to run and secure the site. Some data, such as your IP address, may still be processed by those essential tools, but not for analytics or personalization.

Liferay Platform Consent Preferences

These settings cover cookies and similar technologies used in the platform's administrative areas (managing, configuring, and editing your sites); they do not affect cookies on your end-user-facing sites. Liferay and selected third-party providers may store or read information on your device, such as identifiers, preferences, or session details. Strictly necessary technologies are always on; every other category stays off until you turn it on, and you can change or withdraw your choice at any time from these settings. For the full list of cookies, the third parties involved, and data retention periods, see our Cookie Policy and Privacy Policy

Strictly Necessary

Required for core functions such as signing you in, keeping your session secure, balancing server load, and remembering your consent choices. The platform cannot run without them, so they cannot be turned off. They are not used to track you across other sites or for advertising.

Always Active

Functional

Remember the choices you make, such as language, region, theme, and layout, so the platform behaves the way you set it. First-party only. If turned off, some preferences may reset between visits.

Performance

Collect aggregated, and where possible anonymized, data on how the platform performs, such as page load times, errors, and most-used areas, so we can detect and fix problems. May rely on analytics providers such as Liferay Analytics Cloud. If turned off, issues are detected more slowly.

Personalization

Use your activity in the platform to tailor content, suggestions, and layouts to what is relevant to you. If turned off, you will see more generic, non-personalized content.

Product Analytics

Measure which features are used and how they perform, to guide product decisions and improvements. Uses third-party product-analytics tools such as Pendo, tied to a pseudonymous identifier, and not used for advertising. If turned off, your usage is not included in product analytics.
