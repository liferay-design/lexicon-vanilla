Skins & Accessibility

Press Tab to reveal the Open Accessibility Menu button in the top-left: it is the first keyboard stop on the page. Toggle High Contrast there, and switch Light / Dark with the moon/sun button in the Control Panel header. High Contrast layers on top of whichever mode is active, so you get Light, Light HC, Dark, or Dark HC.
