Skeleton

Source: Lexicon skeleton collection by Diego Alonso. Loading-state placeholders: .sk shapes inside a .skeleton container. The shimmer is painted per shape and every shape shares one viewport-fixed gradient, so the wave sweeps the whole composition as a single front — critical on dark surfaces. Modifiers: .circle, .row, .fade, .surface. Speed: set --skeleton-duration (default 1.6s) or --skeleton-play: paused on any ancestor. Sizes are set inline per shape — they mimic the real component's metrics.
